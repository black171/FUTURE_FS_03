The Experience

Welcome to Tubatse Royal Grill, the heartbeat of Burgersfort. More than just a restaurant, we are a destination where tradition meets premium quality.

Why Our Grill Matters

  • Nutrient-Rich Proteins: Our A-grade meats are flame-grilled to retain essential iron and vitamins, providing a healthy, high-protein meal.
  • Authentic Slow-Cooking: Traditional dishes like our Sunday Mogodu and Hard Body chicken are slow-cooked to preserve deep, natural flavors and tenderness.
  • Fresh Local Sides: From our Chakalaka to our savory Pap, every side is prepared daily using fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

A Place for Connection

  • The Spirit of Ubuntu: Our large sharing platters are designed to be enjoyed together, fostering a sense of community and togetherness.
  • Celebrating Milestones: From graduations to birthdays, we provide the perfect "Royal Vibe" for families to create lasting memories over a shared meal.
  • Unplug and Reconnect: In the warmth of our grill, we encourage guests to put down their phones and engage in the timeless art of table conversation.

"At Tubatse Royal, you don't just eat; you belong."
